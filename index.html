BLACKPINK

Blackpink is a south korean k pop girl group operating under the guidance of YG Entertainment. They consist of 4 members Kim Jisoo,Kim jennie,Park chaeyoung and Lalisa Manoban.
They debuted with their single "Boombayah" on August 2016

Jennie

Her Korean name is Kim Jennie while her stage name is just Jennie. She is member of K-Pop band called Blackpink. Jennie made her solo debut as an artist with the single "solo".

Lalisa

Lalisa Manoban also known as Lisa is member of korean pop band Blackpink. She debuted as an solo artist with single album called Laslisa.
